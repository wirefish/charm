Welcome, Traveler!

You have reached the mystical land of Atalea: a sprawling world full of danger, wonder, and adventure.

Atalea is a multiplayer, text-based adventure game. You play the role of a long-dead hero who has unexpectedly been reborn into the world. What could drive the gods to revive you, pulling you violently from the peaceful rest you have enjoyed for centuries? Start playing now and find out!

You can play the game right here in your browser. If you haven't already, click the button in the upper-right to create an account—it takes just a moment and doesn't require any personal information.

Once you have an account, click the big green button to join the fun!
